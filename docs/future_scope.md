# GeoSentinel AI Future Scope

This document contains ideas, enhancements, and future research directions.

IMPORTANT:

Features listed here are NOT part of the current project scope.

They should only be considered after successful completion of the MVP.

---

## Future Research Directions

---

## 1. LiDAR Integration

Description:
Integrate LiDAR point cloud data for improved terrain and elevation analysis.

Benefits:

- 3D mapping
- Better urban structure analysis
- Terrain modeling

Status:
Future Research

---

## 2. SAR Imagery Support

Description:
Integrate Synthetic Aperture Radar imagery.

Benefits:

- Cloud-independent monitoring

- Improved disaster monitoring

Future Research

## Rejected Ideas

---

## Rule

Before implementing any new feature:

Ask:

1. Does it improve the MVP?
2. Is the current system stable?
3. Are we ahead of schedule?

If any answer is NO:

The idea remains in this document.
