# GeoSentinel AI

## Project Title

GeoSentinel AI: AI-Powered Satellite Change Detection and Urban-Vegetation Risk Intelligence Platform

---

## Problem Statement

Rapid urbanization and environmental degradation are difficult to monitor using traditional geospatial workflows. Manual analysis of satellite imagery is time-consuming, expensive, and difficult to scale.

Organizations require automated systems capable of analyzing multi-temporal satellite imagery, detecting land-use changes, identifying vegetation loss, monitoring urban expansion, and generating actionable geospatial intelligence.

---

## Proposed Solution

GeoSentinel AI is an autonomous GeoAI platform that processes satellite imagery and geospatial data to identify environmental and infrastructural changes over time.

The platform will:

- Ingest multi-temporal Sentinel-2 satellite imagery
- Perform geospatial preprocessing
- Calculate vegetation indices such as NDVI
- Detect urban expansion
- Detect vegetation loss
- Generate risk intelligence reports
- Provide an interactive dashboard
- Support natural language querying through RAG
- Automate workflows using AI agents and pipelines

---
