# Learnings

- Geospatial imagery is raster data.
- Administrative boundaries are vector data.
- CRS alignment is critical for spatial analysis.
- GeoTIFF stores both imagery and geographic metadata.
- Sentinel-2 contains multiple spectral bands.
- NDVI uses Red and Near Infrared bands.
- QGIS is an effective GIS visualization and analysis platform.
- Satellite imagery interpretation requires understanding colors, textures, and patterns.
